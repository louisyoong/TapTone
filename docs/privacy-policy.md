# TapTone Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 1, 2026

TapTone helps colorblind users distinguish colors better. This privacy policy explains how the TapTone mobile app handles information.

## Information TapTone Uses

TapTone may request access to your device camera and photo library so that you can use its assistive filters, live color detector, and color vision simulations.

## Photo And Camera Processing

Photos and live camera images are processed locally on your device. TapTone does not upload, store, sell, or share your photos or camera images.

## Local App Preferences

TapTone stores a small preference on your device to remember whether you completed the onboarding screen. This preference remains on your device and is not sent to a server.

## Data Collection

TapTone does not require an account. TapTone does not collect personal information, analytics, advertising identifiers, location data, or payment information.

## Third-Party Services

TapTone does not include advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

TapTone does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated when TapTone changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when revisions are made.

## Contact

For privacy questions or support, email [louis91work@gmail.com](mailto:louis91work@gmail.com).

## Disclaimer

TapTone is an assistive tool, not a medical device. It helps enhance color differences, but results may vary depending on lighting conditions, camera quality, screen calibration, and individual color vision. TapTone does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. Do not rely on TapTone for medical, safety-critical, or emergency decisions.
