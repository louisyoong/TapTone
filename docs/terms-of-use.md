## Purpose

TapTone is an assistive accessibility tool that helps users distinguish color differences. It includes assistive photo filters, live camera color detection, and color vision simulations.

TapTone is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Results may vary depending on lighting conditions, camera quality, screen calibration, and individual color vision.

## Appropriate Use

Do not rely on TapTone for medical, safety-critical, emergency, or professional decisions where an inaccurate color reading could cause harm or loss. Always use appropriate professional equipment and judgment when accuracy is important.
